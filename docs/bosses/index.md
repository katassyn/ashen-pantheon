# Bosses

Bosses are the heart of Ashen Pantheon. Every major boss is drawn from a real-world mythology and designed to be a memorable, escalating encounter — not a damage sponge with a health bar.

## Design Philosophy

Each boss is built around three principles:

1. **Mythological authenticity** — every boss is rooted in real mythology. Their visual design, abilities, and lore reflect their actual mythological origin.
2. **Multi-phase fights** — bosses evolve as the fight progresses. A boss at 100% health fights differently than the same boss at 20% health.
3. **Learn-able patterns** — bosses are hard but fair. Every attack has a tell. Every mechanic can be understood and countered.

## Mythologies

Ashen Pantheon draws from five real-world mythological traditions:

| Mythology | Region | Themes |
|---|---|---|
| **Greek** | Mediterranean | Hubris, fate, divine punishment |
| **Norse** | Scandinavia | Doom, honor, the end of all things |
| **Egyptian** | North Africa | Judgment, the afterlife, cosmic order |
| **Japanese** | East Asia | Purity, spirits, the boundary between worlds |
| **Slavic** | Eastern Europe | Nature, dark forests, old gods |
