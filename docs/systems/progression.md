# Progression

- **Levels 1–49:** main skill tree, 1 point per level (49 points total).
- **Level 50:** choose one of three ascendancies for your class. Permanent.
- **Levels 51–100:** ascendancy tree, 1 point per 2 levels (25 points total).
- **Level cap:** 100.
