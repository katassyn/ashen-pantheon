# Progression

- **Levels 1–49:** main skill tree, 1 point per level (49 points total).
- **Level 50:** choose one of three ascendancies for your class. Permanent.
- **Levels 51–100:** ascendancy tree, 1 point per 2 levels (25 points total).
- **Level cap:** 100.
- **Attribute points:** 2 per level — freely distributed across Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence. See [Stats & Resistances](stats-and-resistances.md).

## Endgame Resistance Penalties

At levels **50, 75, and 100**, all resistances drop by 20% (cumulative). See [Stats & Resistances](stats-and-resistances.md#resistance-penalties).

| Level Reached | Total Penalty |
|---|---|
| 50 | −20% all res |
| 75 | −40% all res |
| 100 | −60% all res |
