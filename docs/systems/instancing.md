# Instancing

Ashen Pantheon uses a **player-owned instancing system**. Every player controls their own copy of the game world, with the option to invite others.

## Your World Instance

When you log in, you enter your personal instance — a version of the game world that belongs exclusively to you. You control:

- **Progression state** — which bosses you have killed, which zones are cleared
- **Access** — who can enter your world
- **Pace** — there is no server-wide day cycle or shared resource depletion

Nobody can enter your instance without your invitation.

## Party System

You can invite up to **3 other players** to your instance, creating a party of 4.

| Action | How |
|---|---|
| Invite a player | Search by name in the Social panel → Send Instance Invite |
| Accept an invite | Notification appears → Accept to join their instance |
| Leave a party | Party panel → Leave Instance (you return to your own world) |
| Kick a party member | Party leader only — Party panel → Kick Player |

When a party member enters your instance, their progression in **your** world does not affect their own instance. Boss kills in your instance count for you; you must enter their instance for them to get credit.

## Global Layer

Even inside your personal instance, a **global layer** connects you to all players:

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Global Chat** | Persistent text channels shared by all players regardless of instance |
| **Rankings** | Live leaderboards for level, boss kills, and gear score — updated in real time |
| **Trading** | List items for sale and purchase items from other players across instances |

The global layer ensures the game feels like a living world even within solo instancing.

## Boss Instance Scaling

Boss encounters scale based on party size:

| Party Size | Boss HP Multiplier | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1× | Solo tuning — designed to be clearable |
| 2 | 1.6× | Slight increase |
| 3 | 2.1× | Moderate increase |
| 4 | 2.5× | Full party tuning |
