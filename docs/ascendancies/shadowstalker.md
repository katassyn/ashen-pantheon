# Shadowstalker

**Class:** [Archer](../classes/archer.md) · **Role:** Melee Burst Assassin

> *You heard nothing. You saw nothing. You just lost 80% of your health.*

The Shadowstalker is the only Archer ascendancy that fights in melee. They use ranged tools as openers and escape mechanisms, then close in for devastating burst windows amplified by poison and critical hits. Mastering the Shadowstalker means mastering the approach — every engagement should begin and end on your terms.

## Core Mechanics

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Invisibility** | Enter a stealth state that makes you untargetable for a duration or until you attack. Breaking stealth with an attack grants a crit multiplier bonus. |
| **Poison** | A stacking damage-over-time applied by specific skills. Each stack deals independent damage; high stacks deal enormous sustained damage. |
| **Critical Strikes** | Shadowstalker ascendancy nodes dramatically increase critical hit chance and multiplier, especially on the first hit after stealth. |

## Playstyle

Engage from stealth → heavy critical opener → apply poison stacks → retreat to range → re-enter stealth → repeat. The loop rewards players who can manage distance and cooldowns precisely.

## Ascendancy Tree Focus

- Increasing stealth duration and reducing reveal cooldown
- Adding bonuses to the "first hit from stealth" window
- Scaling poison stacks (more stacks, higher individual stack damage)
- Boosting critical hit chance and multiplier passively
- Unlocking a node that refunds stealth cooldown on kill

## Synergies

- Stack **critical hit chance** and **poison damage** on gear.

- Hexblade curses further amplify Shadowstalker poison — a powerful party combo.
