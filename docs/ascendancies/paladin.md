# Paladin

**Class:** [Warrior](../classes/warrior.md) · **Role:** Holy DPS / Party Support

> *Divine fire cleanses what steel cannot.*

The Paladin walks the line between warrior and priest. They deal devastating area-of-effect holy damage, mend wounds with blessed light, and wrap their party in powerful auras that make everyone fight better. The Paladin is the only Warrior ascendancy that meaningfully supports a party, making them one of the most sought-after players in group content.

## Core Mechanics

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **AoE Holy Damage** | Paladin skills release bursts of holy energy in areas around the caster, dealing magic damage based on the character's holy power stat. |
| **Heals** | Targeted and AoE heals restore health to self and party members. Scales with holy power. |
| **Party Aura Buffs** | Persistent auras that radiate from the Paladin and affect all party members within range — boosting damage, defense, or movement speed. |

## Playstyle

Paladin rewards players who can juggle offensive and supportive responsibilities simultaneously. In boss fights, you are managing your aura selection, throwing out heals when party members take spike damage, and weaving in AoE holy bursts on the boss. You are never standing still.

## Ascendancy Tree Focus

Ascendancy tree nodes for Paladin emphasize:

- Expanding AoE radius and damage of holy burst skills
- Increasing heal potency and adding over-time heal effects
- Unlocking additional aura types (offensive, defensive, utility)
- Reducing aura swap cooldown so you can react to changing fight phases
- Adding holy damage bonuses that scale with current mana

## Synergies

- Paladin auras stack with all party member buffs — coordinate with your Berserker for massive damage windows.
- Gear should prioritize **holy power**, **mana pool**, and **cooldown reduction**.
- In solo play, lean into the AoE holy damage build — clear speed compensates for the lack of party synergy.
