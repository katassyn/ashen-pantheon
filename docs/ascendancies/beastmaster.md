# Beastmaster

**Class:** [Archer](../classes/archer.md) · **Role:** Pet Army / Summoner

> *Every myth ever told about a monster? I've tamed one.*

The Beastmaster commands a growing menagerie of animal companions and — uniquely — mythological creatures drawn from the same pantheons as the game's bosses. Where the Arcanist creates elemental constructs, the Beastmaster bonds with living things. Your pets level with you, learn abilities, and can even respond to vocal commands mid-combat.

## Core Mechanics

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Animal Companions** | Natural animals (wolves, eagles, bears) that fight alongside you. Each has unique abilities and a basic AI that can be overridden with commands. |
| **Mythological Companions** | Rarer, more powerful creatures based on mythological beasts — unlocked through specific in-game events. |
| **Companion Synergies** | Certain pet combinations unlock bonus effects (e.g., wolf + eagle = coordinated flanking bonus). |

## Synergies

- Gear that boosts **pet damage**, **pet health**, and **cooldown reduction** maximizes impact.
- In a party, Beastmaster pets can act as secondary tanks, letting Guardian focus on the boss.
- Pairs well with Arcanist — combined pet armies create overwhelming pressure.
