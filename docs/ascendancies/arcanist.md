# Arcanist

**Class:** [Mage](../classes/mage.md) · **Role:** Elemental Summoner / Support

> *Why fight when your creations can fight for you?*

The Arcanist forges living constructs from elemental energy and sends them into battle. Part summoner, part strategist, the Arcanist's power scales with how well you manage your elemental entities — positioning them, directing their abilities, and keeping them alive long enough to matter.

## Core Mechanics

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Elemental Summons** | Conjure persistent entities from Fire, Ice, or Lightning energy. Each type has unique behaviors and abilities. |
| **Pet Micromanagement** | Arcanist pets can be directed to specific targets or positions. Active management dramatically increases their effectiveness. |
| **Support Aura** | Arcanist passively emits an aura that buffs nearby allied pets and party members based on active summon types. |

## Summon Types

| Summon | Element | Behavior |
|---|---|---|
| Flame Wraith | Fire | Aggressive melee attacker, applies burn on hit |
| Frost Construct | Ice | Defensive, taunts enemies away from Arcanist |
| Storm Wisp | Lightning | Ranged, chains lightning between targets |

## Synergies

- Pairs powerfully with Elementalist in a party — Arcanist summons apply elements continuously, setting up reactions for the Elementalist.
- Gear should focus on **summon damage**, **max mana**, and **cooldown reduction**.
- In solo play, build tanky summons to absorb boss attention while you cast from safety.
