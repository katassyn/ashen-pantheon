# Elementalist

**Class:** [Mage](../classes/mage.md) · **Role:** Elemental AoE DPS

> *Why choose one element when you can use all three at once?*

The Elementalist is the apex predator of area-of-effect damage. They do not specialize in a single element — they weaponize the reactions between Fire, Ice, and Lightning to create cascading damage chains that can devastate entire boss phases in seconds.

## Core Mechanics

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Elemental Mixing** | Applying two different elements to the same target creates an elemental reaction (e.g., Fire + Ice = Shatter). Each reaction deals bonus damage and applies a status effect. |
| **AoE Bursts** | Elementalist signature skills release large area bursts of a chosen element, letting you apply elements to entire groups simultaneously. |
| **Elemental Mastery** | Passive stat that increases the damage and effect of all elemental reactions triggered. |

## Elemental Reactions

| Element A | Element B | Reaction | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fire | Ice | Shatter | Massive burst damage + freeze |
| Fire | Lightning | Overload | Chain lightning to nearby enemies |
