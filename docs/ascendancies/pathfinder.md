# Pathfinder

**Class:** [Archer](../classes/archer.md) · **Role:** Tactical Survivalist / Trap Master

> *I was here before the fight started. I'll be here after it ends.*

The Pathfinder is the most self-sufficient character in Ashen Pantheon. They are the tankiest archer build, capable of kiting bosses indefinitely while a network of traps does continuous work. Pathfinders win by attrition — they outlast everything.

## Core Mechanics

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Traps** | Placed objects that trigger on enemy proximity, dealing damage, applying debuffs, or creating terrain effects. Multiple trap types can be active simultaneously. |
| **Kiting** | Pathfinder movement skills are designed for sustained repositioning — keeping enemies at range while maintaining damage output. |
| **Survival** | Pathfinder ascendancy nodes add significant health pool bonuses, damage reduction, and self-healing — making them far tankier than a standard Archer. |

## Trap Types

| Trap | Effect |
|---|---|
| Snare Trap | Roots enemy in place for 2 seconds |
| Explosive Trap | Burst physical damage in an area |
| Venom Trap | Applies poison stacks on trigger |
| Frost Trap | Slows enemy movement speed by 60% |

- Increasing personal health pool and passive damage reduction
- Unlocking a "prepared ground" passive that buffs the Pathfinder's damage while standing in a trap zone

## Synergies

- Stack **trap damage**, **movement speed**, and **health** on gear.
- Pathfinder is the best solo class for learning boss patterns — you can survive long enough to observe every phase.
- In a party, Pathfinder traps provide reliable AoE damage without requiring attention, freeing mental load for mechanics.
