# Guardian

**Class:** [Warrior](../classes/warrior.md) · **Role:** Tank / Aggro Management

> *The line holds because I hold it.*

The Guardian is the definitive tank of Ashen Pantheon. Where the Berserker bleeds and the Paladin heals, the Guardian does the unglamorous but essential work: drawing every threat, absorbing every hit, and making sure their party never has to.

## Core Mechanics

| Mechanic | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Taunt** | Forces nearby enemies and bosses to redirect their attacks to the Guardian for a duration. Essential for party protection. |
| **Party Shields** | Grants temporary absorption shields to party members. Shields absorb a flat amount of damage before health is affected. |
| **Aggro Management** | Passive nodes ensure the Guardian generates more threat than any other party member, keeping enemies focused on them. |

## Playstyle

Guardian is the only ascendancy built around protecting others. In solo play, you are nearly unkillable but kill slowly. In a party, you are the backbone — your Taunt ability lets allies operate freely, your Party Shields buy crucial seconds, and your aggro management ensures the Mage standing in the back never gets touched.

## Ascendancy Tree Focus

Ascendancy tree nodes for Guardian emphasize:

- Increasing Taunt duration and the number of enemies affected
- Strengthening Party Shield absorption values and cooldown reduction
- Passive threat generation multipliers
- Damage reduction while taunting
- Counter-damage — reflecting a portion of absorbed hits back at attackers

## Synergies

- Guardian is most powerful in a 4-player party with at least one healer (Paladin).
- Gear focused on **physical defense**, **health pool**, and **cooldown reduction** maximizes uptime on Taunt.
- In solo play, pair Guardian's defense with the highest damage skills available to compensate for slow clear speed.
