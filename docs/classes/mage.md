# Mage

> *The elements are not forces to be controlled — they are arguments to be won.*

The Mage wields the raw stuff of the cosmos. Where the Warrior wins through strength and the Archer wins through precision, the Mage wins through understanding — reading the battlefield, stacking elemental reactions, and unleashing damage that no armor can fully stop. Mages are fragile but devastating.

## Playstyle

Mages operate from range, managing mana pools and elemental combinations. Your skill tree unlocks elemental affinities, cast speed modifiers, summon slots, and aura nodes. You reward deliberate, thoughtful play over button mashing.

## Ascendancy Paths

At level 50 you choose one of three paths — this choice is **permanent**:

| Ascendancy | Summary |
|---|---|
| [Elementalist](../ascendancies/elementalist.md) | Master of Fire, Ice, and Lightning — elemental mixing and massive AoE bursts |
| [Arcanist](../ascendancies/arcanist.md) | Elemental summoner — conjure entities, micromanage pets, hybrid support |
| [Hexblade](../ascendancies/hexblade.md) | Chaos mage — AoE auras, debilitating curses, damage-over-time |

## Tips

- Mages have low health — invest in magic defense gear and consider movement skills for escape.
- Elementalist builds synergize around applying multiple elements to a single target.
- Hexblade curses stack multiplicatively with DoT — apply the curse first, then the DoT.
