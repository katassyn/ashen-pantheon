# Warrior

> *Where others fall back, the Warrior steps forward.*

The Warrior is the backbone of any party and a relentless solo force. Masters of close-quarters combat, Warriors absorb punishment, dish out crushing physical damage, and bend the flow of battle to their will. Whether you charge into the thick of it, hold the line while allies recover, or channel divine power into holy wrath — the Warrior path is defined by presence.

## Playstyle

Warriors thrive in melee range. Your skill tree grants access to gap closers, crowd control, defensive stances, and raw physical damage multipliers. You are built to be where the fight is.

## Ascendancy Paths

At level 50 you choose one of three paths — this choice is **permanent**:

| Ascendancy | Summary |
|---|---|
| [Berserker](../ascendancies/berserker.md) | Rage-fueled damage dealer — lower HP means higher damage, sustained by lifesteal |
| [Guardian](../ascendancies/guardian.md) | Immovable tank — taunt, party shields, aggro management |
| [Paladin](../ascendancies/paladin.md) | Holy warrior — AoE divine damage, heals, and party-wide aura buffs |

## Tips

- Warriors benefit enormously from gear with high physical defense and bonus health.
- Berserker builds want to stay below 50% HP — plan your lifesteal accordingly.
- Guardian's taunt mechanic is essential in 4-player parties — communicate with your team.
