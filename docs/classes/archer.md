# Archer

> *You never see the shot that kills you.*

The Archer is the most versatile base class — equally comfortable fighting from the treetops, vanishing into shadow, commanding a pack of mythological beasts, or laying a minefield of traps. Archers are mobile, resourceful, and often the most unpredictable player on the battlefield.

## Playstyle

Archers excel at managing distance, using terrain, and maintaining sustained pressure. Your skill tree includes movement skills, pet nodes, trap mechanics, and both melee and ranged damage options — giving you unusual flexibility at the cost of requiring more skill to play optimally.

## Ascendancy Paths

At level 50 you choose one of three paths — this choice is **permanent**:

| Ascendancy | Summary |
|---|---|
| [Shadowstalker](../ascendancies/shadowstalker.md) | Melee burst assassin — invisibility, poison, critical strikes |
| [Beastmaster](../ascendancies/beastmaster.md) | Pet army commander — animal companions and mythological summons |
| [Pathfinder](../ascendancies/pathfinder.md) | Tactical survivalist — tanky kiting, traps, self-sufficiency |

## Tips

- Archers are the only class with access to both melee and ranged damage nodes in the base tree.
- Shadowstalker requires excellent positioning — stealth entry + burst + escape is the full loop.
- Beastmaster scales with the number and quality of active pets; invest in pet stat nodes early.
