# World

The world of Ashen Pantheon is a realm caught between mythological pantheons. Millennia of divine warfare have shattered the natural order — zones that were once distinct regions have collapsed into each other, leaving a fractured landscape where Greek ruins stand beside Norse forests and Egyptian deserts bleed into Slavic marshes.

## The Ashen Realm

The world is called the **Ashen Realm** — named for the divine ash that rains from a sky that has not seen true sunlight since the gods began their war. The ash is not merely atmospheric; it is residual divine energy that empowers those who absorb enough of it. That is what you are: a mortal who has absorbed enough to fight back.

## World Structure

The world is divided into **zones** — distinct geographic regions, each themed around one of the game's mythological traditions. Zones contain:

- Exploration areas with enemies and resources
- Hidden locations with lore items
- One or more **boss arenas** — specially designed encounters that trigger when entered

## Instancing

The world is **player-instanced** — you explore your own copy of the Ashen Realm. See [Instancing](../systems/instancing.md) for full details.

## Global vs. Instance

| Layer | Who sees it | What it contains |
|---|---|---|
| Instance Layer | You (and your party) | Enemies, resources, boss states, your progression |
| Global Layer | All players | Chat, rankings, trading |
